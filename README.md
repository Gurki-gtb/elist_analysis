## Overall Performance  
- In 2019 we generated $3.8M dollars in revenue. In the following years 2020 and 2021, we see a remarkable increase, more than doubling 2019's revenue. With 2020 generating $10.1M and 2021 generating $9.1M. This surge in sales is accredited to the increased online shopping behavior due to the pandemic.
- As the surge in online shopping begins to normalize we also start to see an initial decline in sales during 2021, before stabilzing and at a more consistent level.
- In 2022 we generated $4.9M, this is where the decline of pandemic-driven online shopping momentum becomes increasingly evident. The year starts off relatively strong but sees a gradual decrease as the year goes on and we see our weakest fall/winter holiday season performance yet.
